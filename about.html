About me

I believe in creating a more human and empathetic world.

Prior to being a UX/UI designer, I was an Owners Corporation Manager for a decade. I felt increasingly unfulfilled and craved a change in career.

My “aha moment” emanated when I was dealing with a web development company to develop an app for a building I managed. When I learned about the role of a UX/UI designer, I knew it was something I had to pursue.

I find that the fundamentals of the two industries are quite similar, where one must fulfill many roles and have a user-centric approach to resolve complex issues by coming up with new and innovative solutions.

In my spare time, I am often seen making mixed media sculpture and hiking outdoors.
